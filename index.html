Our Promise

To date, our founders and volunteers have invested unrelenting energy toward helping the Ukrainian people. We vow to continue to aid those refugees still helplessly 'stuck' at the Polish border through regular deliveries. Through our free shop, we will continue to ease life for the Ukrainians newly arriving in our country and to make life for them a little more pleasant. As a donor or sponsor, you can greatly help us in this aim! You will then receive punctual updates on the good your donation is creating. Via this email you can share in the achievements of our association, as well as share them with others.

About us

The “Help Ukraine Lausanne” (HUL) Association was founded by 3 volunteers with a common objective: help Ukrainians affected by the war in Ukraine and the ones arriving in Lausanne who are fleeing their country. Now we are a group of more than 200 volunteers. Our volunteers are of various origins, spanning generations, coming from diverse backgrounds all in a common goal: To work hand-in-hand together to provide as much aid as is possible.
