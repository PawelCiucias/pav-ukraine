We've already come a long way

To do so, the following actions were put in place: a collection point to receive, sort, pack and send donations (including first necessities, medical aids, food, hygiene products, sleeping essentials, and more) directly to the peoples in Ukraine. a distribution point, “The little free shop”, to give clothes, first necessities, food, toys, etc. for free to the Ukrainians fleeing the war and arriving in Lausanne and the region. We also wish to facilitate the integration of families by offering various activities for adults and children in the region. Key achievements

Over 60 tons of essentials delivered to Ukraine

More than 280 volunteers taking action

A 200m2 collection zone organized by our volunteers

A free shop open every day staffed by volunteers

Over 500 Ukranian smiles and counting

Our Promise

We promise to continue to put as much energy as we have invested so far to help the Ukrainians still stuck in the camps at the border and to make life a little more pleasant for the Ukrainians arriving in our country. As a donor or sponsor, you will receive punctual updates via email if you wish to in order to follow the achievement of our association and share them.
